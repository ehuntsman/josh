Beat on the Street

Writers have to pay the bills somehow

Writing is a passion, but not every passion can pay the rent. I’ve been lucky. I was a sports writer for a local newspaper for a few years before transitioning into an “everything” beat. These days, every newspaper writer has to be on the “everything” beat. Sports writing was fun, but I got more money for the “everything” beat.

These days I went corporate and write in the communications department of an engineering firm. We have a trade magazine and it keeps me busy, until one of my several novels get published (estimated publishing date: ???)
